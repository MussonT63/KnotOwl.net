  • The process that Chief Okemos uses... http://knotowl.net/ChiefOkemosDistrict/Advancement/MBCinfo/Process/
    1. The new MBC:
      1. Submits a Merit Badge Councelor Information form.
      2. Is requested to complete training as needed.
      3. Completes training and provides completion documentation.
      4. Is provided the link to the online District Registration.
      5. Submits the registration with District.
    2. The MBC Coordinator:
      1. Approves the District Adult Application (assuming all Yes answers, if not pass it to the Professionals!).
      2. Approves the MBCi form.
      3. eMails the MBCi form to the person that associates MBs to MBCs in Scoutbook (with a Cc:MCC.Registration@scouting.org for archiving).
      4. Communicates back to the new MBC when the process is complete.
      5. Retain paperwork as your District decides.

    5. Existing volunteer, but new Merit Badge Counsleor

      Dear Name,

      I wanted to let you know that I received your merit badge counselor application and YPT certificate and I am the correct person for you to send the form to. Because you are applying as a new merit badge counselor, it is considered a new volunteer position in a new "unit" - in this case the District.

      Even though you are already a registered leader, we need to have a new Adult Volunteer Application filed with the District along with the merit badge application before I can approve you as a merit badge counselor. Please note that since you are already registered there is no cost, but the instructions from the link below can be confusing so once you sign in to my.Scouting.org, then click the link, select Start under Adult, and click Continue on the next page that should ask if you want to be registered in both units. Complete the form and submit.

      This volunteer form can be completed online at:
      my.Scouting.org/(District Specific link)

      If you believe you have already done this, please let me know.
