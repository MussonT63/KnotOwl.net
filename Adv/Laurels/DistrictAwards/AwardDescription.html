Charter Org/Org Rep of the Year The award is presented to a chartered partner or Chartered Org Representative who demonstrates outstanding or distinctive relationships that go beyond the normal expectations.
Chartered Partner Award
Community Partner of the Year Presented to a community leader or organization who has provided outstanding support to the Scouting program in our district. The community leader does not have to be a registered scout
Community Leadership Award
Crew Advisor of the Year Presented to an Crew Leader that has shown exemplary adult support to a Scouting Adventure Crew.
Crew of the Year Presented to an Adventure Crew that has coordinated an outstanding Scouting program during the past year.
Post Advisor of the Year Presented to a Post Leader that has shown exemplary adult support to a Scouting Explorerĺs Post.
Post of the Year Presented to an Explorer Post that has coordinated an outstanding Scouting program during the past year.
Scoutmaster of the Year Presented to a Troop Leader that has shown exemplary adult support to a Scouting Troop.
Troop of the Year Presented to a Scout Troop that has implemented an outstanding Scouting program during the past year.
Cubmaster of the Year Presented to a Pack Leader that has shown exemplary adult support to a Cub Scouting program.
Pack of the Year Presented to an Cub Scout Pack that has implemented an outstanding Scouting program during the past year.
Committee Chair of the Year To recognize a Troop or Pack Committee Chair that has provided outstanding support to a Pack or Troop.
Den Chief of the Year Recognizing a Scouting Den Chief that has provided outstanding support to a Cub Scouting Den/Pack.
Jack/Jill of all Trades This Scouter is never afraid to take on a task, no matter how big or small, if the Scouts benefit from his or her labor and devotion.
Rookie of the Year Presented to a first or second year leader who has shown great initiative within their scouting unit and jumped into Scouts with both feet.
Family of the Year Presented to a family who, as a group, has provided outstanding support to the Scout program in our District. Those families that make Scouting a "family commitment" and priority.
Bill Ames/Youth Scout Spirit Award Given to a Scouting Youth that best demonstrates the Scout Oath and Law in their daily actions.
Bill Ames/Adult Scout Spirit Award Given to a Scouting Adult that best demonstrates and teaches their youth the spirit of Scouting.
Popcorn Kernel of the Year Given to a Scouting Adult who coordinates an exemplary Popcorn Sales program in their Scouting Unit.
Den Chief of the Year Awarded to a Youth Den Chief who has demonstrated a high level of achievement in assisting a Den/Pack in their duties.
Special Recognition We also would like to recognize any individual(s) that you believe deserves recognition but may not fit in one of the above categories. Create an award and submit with the information about the recipient.
